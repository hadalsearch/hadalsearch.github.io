Chrome:
1) Open Google Chrome
2) In the top right, click the three dots and then settings. Or in your address bar enter chrome://settings
3) Under "Search Engine" click "Manage Search Engines"
4) Search for "hadal", click the three dots, and then "Make Default".
If hadal is not showing up, click the "Add" button and enter for the URL: https://hadal.io/results.html?searchq=%s&filter=founder
